# RestroPOS Outreach Playbook
*A jargon-free, high-conversion outbound strategy for LinkedIn, direct email, and live sales calls.*

---

## 1. LinkedIn Outreach Sequence

Targeting Owners, Founders, Partners, and General Managers of independent restaurants, cafes, and bars.

### Step 1: The Connection Request (Max 300 chars)
*Goal: Build curiosity and context. Zero sales pitch.*

> **Option A (Cafe/Restaurant Focus):**
> Hi [First Name], noticed you run [Restaurant Name] in [City]. Love the vibe there. I built a local POS system that gets rid of monthly billing software subscriptions entirely. Would love to connect and follow your growth.
>
> **Option B (Direct/Bold):**
> Hi [First Name], quick question: are you also tired of Toast/Petpooja charging you ₹25k/year just to keep your order screens turned on? I built an alternative where you buy the code once and own it forever. Let’s connect.

## 3. The 10-Minute Sales Demo Script

### Phase 1: Disarm & Validate (Minutes 0 - 3)
- **Goal:** Get them talking about their pain points.
- **Script:** 
  > *"Thanks for taking the call, [Name]. I promise to keep this under 10 minutes. First, tell me: what POS are you running right now at [Restaurant Name], and what's the single most annoying thing about it? Is it the monthly software bill, slow support, or the fact that you have to pay extra just to see your inventory reports?"*
- **Action:** Listen carefully. Note down the specific modules they complain about (e.g. attendance, inventory, printer bugs).

### Phase 2: The Live Interface (Minutes 3 - 7)
- **Goal:** Show them how fast it runs. Open `restropos.vercel.app`.
- **Script:** 
  > *"Let's look at the actual system. Here is the cashier terminal. Notice how simple it is: it's not bloated with 50 tabs you'll never use. 
  > 1. Select the table (T5).
  > 2. Add Butter Chicken and Naan.
  > 3. Click Pay. The receipt is printed, sales logged. Under 3 seconds.
  > 
  > If a customer wants to self-order, they scan this QR code. It takes them to this interface (show mobile cart tab). They place the order, and it pops up on the kitchen printer instantly. No commissions to aggregators."*

### Phase 3: The Custom Code & Math (Minutes 7 - 9)
- **Goal:** Prove the "Ownership" math and configure their custom package live on the configurator.
- **Script:** 
  > *"Now here is the configurator (scroll to `#pos-builder`). We don't believe in locking features behind high tiers. Our basic package is ₹14,999. If you don't need Employee Attendance, you don't pay for it. 
  > Let's select what you need: you mentioned you want Inventory Tracking (+₹5,000) and QR Table Ordering (+₹5,000). Your total upfront code fee is ₹24,999 once. 
  > 
  > From next year onwards, you pay ₹0/month for the software. You only pay us a flat ₹999/year maintenance fee to secure your database backups. You save over ₹50,000 in the next three years."*

### Phase 4: The Call to Action (Minutes 9 - 10)
- **Goal:** Get their menu and schedule setup.
- **Script:** 
  > *"Our next step is simple. If you want to move forward, we don't ask for a credit card. Just WhatsApp or email me your current menu card. Our engineering team will load it into your test build, set up your brand colors, and send you a link to log in. You check it out, see if you like it, and only pay when you're ready to deploy. 
  > Shall I register your email for a test compilation?"*
